# Boss Arena

The corridor opens into a vast underground amphitheater that dwarfs everything else in the depths. The ceiling vaults upward into darkness, supported by massive pillars carved to resemble robed figures with their arms raised overhead. A ring of braziers circles the central floor, their flames burning an unnatural blue-white that illuminates carved channels in the stone -- channels that once carried something, though they are dry now. The center of the arena bears a mosaic of concentric circles in alternating stone and dark earth, unmistakably a ritual space. The air thrums with residual energy, and the walls seem to absorb sound, creating an eerie hush that makes even breathing feel intrusive.
