# The Seat of Stillness

The Preserver Commander's throne room. A cavernous hall where every footstep echoes off vaulted stone ceilings. The throne itself is carved from a single block of grey marble, unadorned except for the Preserver sigil etched into the headrest -- a closed eye wreathed in thorns. Banners in muted purple and silver hang from iron rods along the walls, their edges frayed from years of neglect. The Commander has not held court in a long time. The air tastes of dust and cold iron.
