# The Hollow Court

An open courtyard at the heart of the Preserver Fortress, ringed by the inner walls. A stone fountain stands at the center, dry -- its basin cracked, its spout crusted with mineral deposits from whatever water last flowed through it. Paving stones radiate outward in concentric circles, though weeds have forced their way through the joints in places where the Preservers have stopped bothering to maintain. Training dummies and weapon racks line the eastern wall. A market stand with wilting produce sits near the well, more from habit than any real commerce. The courtyard feels too large for the handful of Preservers who still use it, their footsteps swallowed by all that empty stone.
