# The Sanctum of Preservation

The deepest chamber of the fortress, where the Preserver Commander makes their last stand. A vast circular arena carved from living rock, its floor inlaid with a mosaic of the Preserver sigil -- the closed eye, rendered in dark and light stone, spanning nearly the entire room. Four massive columns support a domed ceiling lost in shadow. Torchlight doesn't reach the top; it just fades into black. Memory fragments drift through the air like dust motes, drawn to this place from across the fortress. The Commander stands at the far end, arms folded, waiting. They've been waiting for a long time. The mosaic eye on the floor seems to follow your movement as you cross the threshold.
