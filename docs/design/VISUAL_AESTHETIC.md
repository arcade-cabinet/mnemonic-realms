# Visual Aesthetic Guide

## Overview

**Mnemonic Realms** embraces a **Sword & Sorcery / High Fantasy** aesthetic, anchored in medieval themes with dark, gritty undertones. The visual style draws inspiration from classic fantasy literature, tabletop RPGs, and isometric computer RPGs of the late 90s.

## Design Philosophy

### Core Themes

1. **Ancient & Weathered** - Everything feels aged, used, battle-worn
2. **Medieval & Mystical** - Castles, swords, sorcery, dragons
3. **Dark & Dramatic** - Heavy shadows, dramatic lighting, ominous atmospheres
4. **Earthy & Realistic** - Natural colors, leather, stone, metal, wood
5. **Heroic & Epic** - Grand adventures, legendary artifacts, mythical creatures

### Inspiration Sources

#### Literature & Media
- **Conan the Barbarian** (Robert E. Howard)
  - Raw, gritty, primal strength
  - Ancient civilizations and forgotten ruins
  - Sword-wielding warriors in savage lands

- **Dungeons & Dragons**
  - Classic medieval fantasy tropes
  - Wizards, warriors, rogues, clerics
  - Dungeon crawling and treasure hunting

- **Lord of the Rings** (J.R.R. Tolkien)
  - Epic quests and world-ending threats
  - Deep lore and ancient magic
  - Diverse races and cultures

- **Warhammer Fantasy**
  - Dark medieval aesthetic
  - Gritty, brutal combat
  - Corrupting dark forces

#### Video Games
- **Baldur's Gate (1998)** - Isometric D&D RPG
- **Diablo (1996)** - Dark gothic dungeon crawler
- **Icewind Dale (2000)** - Frozen northern adventures
- **Planescape: Torment (1999)** - Dark, philosophical fantasy

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

#### Blood Red / Burgundy
- **Hex**: `#8b1f1f` (primary-500)
- **Usage**: Warrior themes, danger, combat, blood
- **Represents**: Courage, battle, sacrifice, violence
- **Applications**: Primary buttons, warrior class, health bars

#### Ancient Bronze / Copper
- **Hex**: `#b8860b` (secondary-500)
- **Usage**: Metallic accents, legendary items, ancient artifacts
- **Represents**: Age, value, craftsmanship, treasure
- **Applications**: Borders, icons, legendary loot, coins

#### Deep Forest Green
- **Hex**: `#1f4d2a` (tertiary-500)
- **Usage**: Nature, wilderness, rangers, druids
- **Represents**: Life, growth, natural magic, forests
- **Applications**: Nature-themed content, ranger class, forest biomes

#### Bright Gold
- **Hex**: `#d4af37` (alignment.light)
- **Usage**: Divine magic, holy power, treasure, heroism
- **Represents**: Divinity, purity, wealth, heroism
- **Applications**: Cleric/paladin classes, legendary items, quest rewards

### Background Colors

#### Leather Brown
- **From**: `#1a1410` - Deep aged leather

- **Usage**: Main background gradients
- **Texture**: Worn leather, ancient scrolls, weathered wood

#### Stone Gray
- **Hex**: `#4a4540`
- **Usage**: UI panels, dungeon walls, rocky terrain
- **Texture**: Rough stone, castle walls, cave surfaces

#### Aged Parchment
- **Hex**: `#f5f1e8`
- **Usage**: Text areas, scrolls, readable surfaces
- **Texture**: Old paper, maps, ancient tomes

## Typography

### Font Families

#### Display Font: "Cinzel Decorative"
- **Weight**: 900 (black)
- **Usage**: Main title, logo, chapter headings
- **Characteristics**: Ornate, medieval, decorative capitals
- **Fallback**: "Crimson Text", serif

#### Heading Font: "Cinzel"
- **Weights**: 400 (normal), 700 (bold), 900 (black)
- **Usage**: Section headings, UI labels, button text
- **Characteristics**: Clean medieval serif, highly readable
- **Fallback**: "Crimson Text", serif

#### Body Font: "Merriweather"
- **Weights**: 400 (normal), 700 (bold)
- **Usage**: Paragraphs, dialogue, descriptions
- **Characteristics**: Classic serif, excellent readability
- **Fallback**: "Georgia", serif

#### Monospace Font: "Courier New"
- **Usage**: Stats, numbers, code-like content
- **Characteristics**: Fixed-width, technical feel
- **Fallback**: monospace

### Typography Scale

```
xs:   0.75rem   (12px) - Small labels, metadata
sm:   0.875rem  (14px) - Body text, descriptions
base: 1rem      (16px) - Standard text
lg:   1.125rem  (18px) - Emphasized text
xl:   1.25rem   (20px) - Small headings
2xl:  1.5rem    (24px) - Section headings
3xl:  1.875rem  (30px) - Major headings
4xl:  2.25rem   (36px) - Page titles
5xl:  3rem      (48px) - Hero text, logo
```

## UI Elements

### Borders & Frames

- **Style**: Angular, squared corners (medieval architecture)
- **Thickness**: 2-4px for emphasis
- **Material**: Bronze, steel, aged wood
- **Decoration**: Simple geometric patterns, no curves
- **Radius**: Minimal (0-0.75rem) for angular medieval feel

### Shadows & Depth

- **Style**: Deep, heavy shadows for dramatic effect
- **Intensity**: Higher opacity (0.4-0.8) than modern UI
- **Glow Effects**: Red/bronze glows for magical elements
- **Layer Separation**: Clear z-index hierarchy

### Textures

#### Primary Textures
1. **Leather** - UI backgrounds, panels, buttons
2. **Stone** - Walls, floors, structural elements
3. **Metal** - Borders, accents, weapons, armor
4. **Wood** - Frames, tables, furniture
5. **Parchment** - Text areas, maps, scrolls

#### Application
- Use subtle noise/grain for authenticity
- Avoid flat colors - add depth with gradients
- Weathering effects - scratches, wear, age
- Lighting - consider torch/firelight sources

### Iconography

- **Style**: Simple, bold, medieval-themed
- **Examples**: Swords, shields, scrolls, potions, coins
- **Source**: Lucide React icons adapted to theme
- **Custom**: Fantasy-specific icons where needed

## Animation & Motion

### Transitions

- **Speed**: Slightly slower (200-600ms) for epic feel
- **Easing**: Cubic-bezier for smooth, weighty movement
- **Style**: Heavy, deliberate, not snappy

### Effects

- **Glow**: Magic items, active elements, hover states
- **Fade**: Subtle transitions, appearing/disappearing
- **Slide**: Panels, modals, menus
- **Shake**: Impact, critical hits, errors

### Particle Effects (Future)

- **Fire**: Torches, magic, dragon breath
- **Blood**: Combat impacts, critical hits
- **Sparks**: Metal clashing, enchantments
- **Smoke**: Potions, spells, atmosphere

## Terminology & Voice

### Word Choices

Replace modern/technical terms with fantasy equivalents:

| Modern | Fantasy |
|--------|---------|
| Game | Quest / Adventure / Saga |
| Save | Chronicle / Record |
| Load | Resume / Continue |
| New Game | New Quest / Begin Journey |
| Settings | Options / Preferences |
| Player | Hero / Adventurer / Champion |
| Start | Embark / Begin |
| Click | Choose / Select |

### Writing Style

- **Formal**: Use archaic/formal language where appropriate
- **Immersive**: Stay in-world, avoid breaking fourth wall
- **Descriptive**: Paint vivid pictures with words
- **Epic**: Grand language for grand adventures

### Example Text

**Modern**: "Click to start a new game"
**Fantasy**: "Speak the words of power to begin your quest"

**Modern**: "Save your progress"
**Fantasy**: "Record your tale in the chronicles"

**Modern**: "Error: Invalid input"
**Fantasy**: "The runes resist your incantation"

## Responsive Design

### Breakpoints

```
Mobile:   < 640px  (sm) - Compact, single column
Tablet:   640-1024px (md-lg) - Two columns, reduced spacing
Desktop:  > 1024px (xl) - Full layout, maximum spacing
```

### Mobile Considerations

- Larger touch targets (48px minimum)
- Simplified textures (performance)
- Readable fonts (no tiny medieval script)
- Reduced shadows/effects (battery)

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast

- Maintain WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for text)
- Text on leather: Use light parchment (#f5f1e8)
- Text on stone: Use gold/bronze for visibility
- Never sacrifice readability for aesthetics

### Font Sizes

- Minimum 14px for body text
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Scalable for user preferences

## Conclusion

The sword & sorcery aesthetic of **Mnemonic Realms** creates an immersive, dark fantasy experience that honors the genre's roots while providing a modern, accessible interface. Every visual choice reinforces the medieval fantasy theme, from the aged leather textures to the dramatic shadows that evoke torch-lit dungeons and ancient ruins.

By maintaining consistency in color, typography, and visual language, we create a cohesive world that players can lose themselves in, seed after seed, adventure after adventure.
